Our Vision & Commitment

Our Vision is to encourage the development of football at all levels, with the emphasis of giving priority to the communities on the Cape Flats.

Our commitment is to use football as a vehicle to build young people’s self-confidence, self-worth and self-esteem. Develop team work and leadership skills. Help young people discover purpose and set life goals. Encourage respect for the opposition and others in general, include players of all abilities and skill sets but above all to have fun.

Our History

First National Football Club was Formed in 1977 and played in the Cape Western Leagues Division.

RANGERS FC Formed in 1947 by M.O.T.H and had its home ground at Malta Park, Observatory until the clubhouse was demolished by the developer of the Adidas Complex at Fir Street, Observatory with permission from the City of Cape Town. Promises to build the clubhouse at another location at Malta Park has up to today not materialised hence the waste of the Complex.

ST AGNES FC Formed in 1948 in Woodstock and had its home at Malta Park.

FIRST NATIONAL RANGERS ST AGNES FC Formed in 1993 and currently plays in the Cape Town Tygerberg LFA under the auspices of SAFA Cape Town.
